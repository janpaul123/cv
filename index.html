Curriculum Vitae: Jan Paul Posma

  • Education

      MSc in Computer Science, University of Oxford

      I am currently reading Computer Science at the University of Oxford, where I am working on an interactive online programming course aimed at high school students.

    • 2008 – 2011

      BSc in Computer Science, University of Groningen

      I have completed the Computer Science bachelor at the University of Groningen with honours, cum laude. For my bachelor thesis I have researched new ways of editing wikis such as Wikipedia. In 2011 development continued in close collaboration with developers of GRNET, a Greek government-funded research institute. The final thesis can be found online.
